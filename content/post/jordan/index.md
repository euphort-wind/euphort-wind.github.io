Hej! I'm sharing a glimpse of my experience visiting ancient Jordan. I was there in 2011. Below are some notes on what I tasted, saw and listened to.

<b>Important:</b> I only share my own experience and recommend the things I personally tried and liked. Therefore, everything written below is extremely subjective, and does not cover all the wonderful local features and places ☺️.

<b>🗺 Places:</b> Petra, Aqaba.<br>

<b>🏛 Top sights visited: </b>

{{< spoiler text="Click to view" >}}
1. Petra
    - The Siq <i>(السيق)</i>
    - Al-Khazneh Treasury <i>(الخزنة)</i>
    - The Urn Tombs <i>(قبر الجرة)</i>
    - Petra Theater <i>(مسرح البتراء)</i>
    - The "Street of Facades" <i>(شارع الواجهات)</i>
2. Aqaba
    - Aqaba Flagpole <i>(سارية العلم العقبة)</i>
    - Aqaba Harbor <i>(ميناء العقبة)</i>
    - Al-Sharif Al-Hussein Bin Ali Mosque <i>(مسجد الشريف الحسين بن علي في العقبة)</i>
{{< /spoiler >}}

- 🇯🇴 Diana Karazon (singer). Genre: pop

<b>🎚️ Authentic EDM genres: </b>
- Shamstep. Originated: mid-2010s


<b>🥘 Local food & drinks: </b>
- Hummus <i>(حمص بطحينة)</i>
- Makmoura <i>(مكمورة)</i>
- Qalayet Bandora <i>(قلاية البندورة)</i>
- Mansaf <i>(منسف)</i>
- Zarb-Style Lamb <i>(زرب)</i>
- Warbat <i>(وربات)</i>
- Knafeh <i>(كنافة)</i>
- Khubz <i>(خبز)</i>
- Arabic Tea <i>(شَايْ عَرَبي)</i>



<b>🎬 Local movies:</b>
- Captain Abu Raed <i>(كابتن أبو رائد)</i>. Year: 2007. Genre: drama


<b>🪔 Authentic features:</b>
- The Tradition of Al-Mansaf Festive Banquet <i>(وليمة المنسف التقليدية)</i> 
- The Dabke Dance <i>(دبكة)</i>
- Authentic Musical Instrument: Mijwiz <i>(مجوز)</i>
- Authentic Garb: Dishdashah & Thagiyah <i>(الدشداشة والطاقية)</i>
- The Culture of Bedouins <i>(بَدَوِيُّون / بَدْو)</i> 
- The Legacy of Nabataeans <i>(𐢕𐢃𐢋𐢈 / الأنباط)</i> 
  - The Nabataean Religion <i>(ديانة نبطية)</i> 
  - The Nabataean Script <i>(أبجدية نبطية)</i> 
  - The Nabataean Betyls <i>(حجر مقدس نبطي)</i> 


<b>🎊 Authentic holidays & celebrations:</b>
- Independence Day of Jordan <i>(الاستقلال)</i>. Month: May
- <u>Ma'an (Regional Special):</u> Petra Cultural Festival <i>(مهرجان البترا الثقافي)</i>. Month: October



<b>💐 National flower: </b> Black Iris <i>(السوسنة السوداء)</i>


<b>🦉 Local wisdom:</b> "Whoever knocks on the door gets an answer." / "<i>اللي بدقّ الباب بسمع الجواب</i>" — Jordanian proverb 


<b>👨‍🎓 The oldest university:</b> The University of Jordan <i>(الجامعة الأردنية)</i>, Amman, est. 1962.
